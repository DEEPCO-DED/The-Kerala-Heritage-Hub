Frequently Asked Questions

What is The Kerala Heritage Hub?

The Kerala Heritage Hub is a digital platform dedicated to preserving, promoting, and celebrating the rich cultural, historical, and artistic legacy of Kerala. It serves as a one-stop resource for exploring Kerala’s traditions, architecture, festivals, and local stories.

What kind of content can I find on the platform?

You can explore detailed articles, documentaries, virtual tours, photo galleries, interviews with artisans, festival highlights, and research-based insights into Kerala’s cultural heritage and art forms.

Who can use The Kerala Heritage Hub?

The platform is open to everyone—students, researchers, travelers, history enthusiasts, and cultural organizations interested in learning about Kerala’s heritage and traditions.

Is the platform free to access?

Yes, most sections of The Kerala Heritage Hub are freely accessible. However, premium content such as curated documentaries, expert-led workshops, and research resources may require a subscription or one-time access fee.

Can users contribute their own content?

Absolutely! We welcome local historians, photographers, storytellers, and researchers to submit articles, photographs, or videos that align with our mission to preserve Kerala’s heritage.

Does The Kerala Heritage Hub collaborate with cultural institutions?

Yes. We partner with museums, universities, tourism boards, and art institutions to curate authentic and educational content that reflects Kerala’s diverse cultural identity.

Is there an offline or mobile app version available?

A mobile-friendly version is available for all devices, and an app is currently under development to allow offline access to selected content and personalized learning experiences.

How does The Kerala Heritage Hub support local artisans?

We feature artisan stories, promote handcrafted products through virtual exhibitions, and provide visibility to local craftspeople by connecting them with global audiences and buyers.

Can I visit physical heritage sites listed on the platform?

Yes. Each listed site includes detailed location data, visiting guidelines, and historical context, allowing visitors to plan in-person experiences while respecting cultural and environmental guidelines.

How can I get involved or support the project?

You can volunteer, donate, collaborate as a content contributor, or participate in our cultural programs. Visit the “Get Involved” section to learn more about partnership opportunities.
