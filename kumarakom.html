Kumarakom

Kumarakom, located on the banks of Vembanad Lake in Kerala, is a tranquil haven of backwaters, lush paddy fields,Kumarakom is a cluster of small islands on the Vembanad Lake, Kerala’s largest lake. Known for its tranquil backwaters, it offers an unmatched experience of nature and relaxation. The village is surrounded by lush paddy fields, coconut groves, and calm waters, making it perfect for those looking to escape city life. and swaying coconut palms. Famous for its houseboats, visitors can glide through the calm waters, witnessing the everyday life of village communities and the rich flora and fauna along the shores. The Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary attracts migratory birds, making it a paradise for birdwatchers and nature enthusiasts. With its peaceful ambiance, beautiful sunsets, and traditional Kerala experiences, Kumarakom is the perfect escape for relaxation and rejuvenation.
